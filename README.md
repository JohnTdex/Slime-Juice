
Slime Juice
================

Ever had a stack of slime balls just laying around on a chest and couldn't figure out what to do with it other than making sticky piston/slime blocks?
Well look no further! 

Slime Juice mod adds an edible and drinkable items called Slime Cube and Slime Juice that has a chance of getting
either a jump boost, speed boost or... BOTH!

================================

Created by yours truly, Lyron18th.
Hope you enjoy this simple mod!
